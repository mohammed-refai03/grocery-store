Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our fresh delivery service.

What are your delivery hours?

We deliver 7 days a week, from 6:00 AM to 11:00 PM. Orders placed after 10:30 PM will be scheduled for the next morning's first slot.

Is there a minimum order value?

Yes, the minimum order for Express Delivery is $15. For orders over $50, we offer completely free delivery!

How do you ensure the freshness of produce?

Our "Fresh-Pick" protocol means we source vegetables daily from local farms. Perishables are stored and transported in UV-sanitized, temperature-controlled bags to maintain the cold chain.

What is your return policy?

We have a "No-Questions-Asked" return policy at the doorstep. If you aren't satisfied with any item's quality upon arrival, simply hand it back to the rider for an instant refund to your FreshMart wallet.

Do you deliver to my area?

Currently, we serve all major city centers and surrounding suburbs. You can enter your zip code on our homepage to check specific availability for your street.
